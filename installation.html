Installation Guide

Easy install. Stunning result.

Mount with construction silicone in 30 minutes, or screw into studs for a permanent finish. Add LED strips to backlight the panels. No special tools required.

Two proven methods

Pick your install approach.

Method 1 · Silicone Adhesive

The 30-minute wall.

Apply construction-grade silicone (we recommend Loctite PL Premium) in vertical zig-zag beads across the back of each panel. Press onto clean drywall. Hold for 60 seconds — done.

  • ✓ No drilling, no studs required
  • ✓ Fastest method — 30–45 min per wall
  • ✓ Removable with effort if you ever move
  • ✓ Perfect for renters with landlord approval
Method 2 · Screw Mount

Permanent and rock-solid.

Drive 1.5" drywall screws through the felt backing into studs or drywall anchors. The felt hides the screw heads — no putty, no touch-ups needed.

  • ✓ Best for commercial and high-traffic spaces
  • ✓ Screws hidden inside felt — invisible finish
  • ✓ Panels come off cleanly if needed
  • ✓ Preferred by contractors and installers
Step by step

Install in four steps.

Measure & Plan

Measure your wall and divide by 24 inches — that's how many panels you'll need. Mark a level line at the top and use a laser for alignment.

Prep the Surface

Wipe down the wall. For silicone, make sure the drywall is dust-free. For screws, locate your studs with a stud finder.

Mount the Panels

Apply silicone or drive screws at top, bottom, and every 24 inches along the panel. Work from one corner outward.

Finish & Trim

Cut end panels to size with a circular saw. Add optional LED strip behind the top edge for a backlit glow.

Add-on

Backlit panels with LED strips.

Want that hotel-lobby look? Add a warm-white LED strip above, below, or behind your panels. Peel, stick, plug in. The glow bounces off the wood for a premium ambient effect.

  • ✦ 2700K warm white — matches the wood tones
  • ✦ 16 ft strip covers a full feature wall
  • ✦ Dimmable transformer included
  • ✦ Plug-in — no electrician required
