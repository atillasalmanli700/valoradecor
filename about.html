Our story

Modern materials, made accessible.

Valora Decor was founded in Alpharetta with a simple idea: high-end acoustic wall panels shouldn't be reserved for luxury hotels. They should be within reach of every homeowner, contractor, and designer in Atlanta.

Why we started

A wall is never just a wall.

We spent years working with architects and designers across metro Atlanta. One pattern kept repeating: clients loved the wood-paneled walls they saw in boutique hotels, but the pricing from European brands was unreachable, and local suppliers kept stock too thin for real projects.

So we built Valora Decor. Bigger panels. Real wood. Acoustic backing included. Contractor pricing from day one. And a physical showroom where you can actually touch what you're buying.

Everything we stock is sitting in our Alpharetta warehouse today. No 12-week lead times. No ocean-freight drama. Just walls that make rooms feel designed.

What we believe

Four principles.

01

Real materials.

Every panel uses genuine wood veneer. Plastic laminates pretend to be wood for a while, then crack, yellow, and peel. We don't ship that.

02

Local stock.

Everything we sell is in our warehouse today. Same-week pickup in Alpharetta, next-week delivery across metro Atlanta.

03

Fair pricing.

Designer-brand quality at contractor-friendly prices. Starting at $69 per panel — roughly half of what import brands charge.

04

Personal service.

No call centers. When you call Valora, you reach Atilla or Steve — the two people who can actually answer your question.
